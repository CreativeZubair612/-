Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 3-7 business days depending on complexity. Simple logo designs might take 2-3 days, while complete branding packages can take 2-3 weeks. I'll provide a specific timeline when you submit your project brief.

How many revisions are included?

Each package includes 2-3 rounds of revisions. If you need more revisions, we can discuss additional charges. I always ensure you're happy with the final design!

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive designs in multiple formats including PNG, JPG, PDF, and editable source files (PSD/AI). This ensures you can use your designs for any purpose.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and credit cards. A 50% deposit is required to start the project, with the remaining balance due upon completion.

Can you work with my brand guidelines?

Absolutely! If you have existing brand guidelines, color schemes, or style preferences, please share them with me. I'll ensure all designs align perfectly with your brand identity.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Yes! I can prioritize rush projects for an additional fee. Contact me to discuss your timeline and I'll let you know if I can accommodate your needs.
